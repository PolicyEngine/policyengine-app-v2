Methodology

This analysis uses the PolicyEngine UK microsimulation model to estimate the impact of relaxing the salary sacrifice pension cap from £2,000 (Autumn Budget 2025 current law) to £5,000. For detailed methodology on how we model salary sacrifice, see our research article. The code used to generate these results is also available.
